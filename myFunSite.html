About WanderLust

We are a company founded by people who love to travel. Whether it is for a relaxing night under the open stars or visiting a chic winery in a cute town, we have the connections to customize the experience for our customers. We have learned from our own travel experiences, and practiced the best tips and tricks for a smooth and fun adventure. Let us take care of guiding your trip, so you can follow the call of adventure, and feed your WanderLust.

Our CEO was born in Louisiana but grew up in Washington. Together with her friends, she has explored the best campsites ranging from the cheapest to the most expensive options. On weekends, she travels on day trips to small towns and big cities in and around Washington, exploring the respective cultures. She felt a tug on her heart and a pull on her feet to go out and explore the hiking trails, camping sites, lakes, and towns around Washington, devoting her vacation time to exploring the rest of the world. She fed her WanderLust, is it time for you to feed yours?
